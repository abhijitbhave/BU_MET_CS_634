About James Ruiz

James Ruiz is an emerging American singer-songwriter, musician from Phoenix, Arizona. Ruiz performs his work primarily in soft rock and acoustic genres.

Early Life

James was born and raised in Phoenix, Arizona. He attended Desert Chapel High School in Phoenix, Arizona, then Biola College. James started singing in while in middle school and in the high school christian choir.

Early Life - continued

James was a pitcher in high school and went on to the Biola College Fighting Eagles baseball team where he earned a varsity letter and led his team to the Division VI divisional playoffs. However, his pursuit of a professional baseball career was cut short by an elbow injury in his senior year of college. James instead pursued his other passion, his music, writing songs while attending college.

Music Career

James has been writing music since college at 21 years of age. Mr. Ruiz credits David Crowder, Dustin Blatnik, Kaleigh Schriner, Bethel Worship, and Adam Lazzara for his inspiration and his particular style of music. James has collaborated with musicians such as Ryan De Champ and Art Jacobo and posted two videos for his latest singles, Glorious and New Fire. James Ruiz is currently playing in small venues throughout Phoenix and Southern California with Ryan De Champ and Monique.
